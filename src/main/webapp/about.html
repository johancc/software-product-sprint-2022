Our Mission

Improving accessibility for all

Our Help is dedicated to providing a platform that helps connect people in need to nearby resources through an easy ZIP Code search. We at Our Help recognize that there are an abundance of resources that are readily available, but many are not always easy to find. We have built a platform that allows anyone to search for programs, resources, and organizations that help improve their quality of life.
